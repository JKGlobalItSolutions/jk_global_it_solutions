Support Features

Comprehensive cybersecurity solutions to protect your organization

Identity & Access Management (IAM)

JK Global IT Solutions improves digital security with IAM strategies, multi-factor authentication, and identity governance solutions. We monitor and prevent unauthorized access.

Data Protection

JK Global IT Solutions offers robust data security measures, including encryption, classification frameworks, secure storage and backups, data retention policies, and disposal practices to prevent breaches.

Threat Detection & Response

JK Global IT Solutions provides security solutions that integrate advanced monitoring tools and technologies for proactive threat detection. We offer real-time alerts, incident response planning, and vulnerability assessments.

User Awareness & Training

JK Global IT Solutions offers customizable security awareness programs, training modules, and simulated phishing campaigns to mitigate social engineering and phishing risks.

Privileged Access Management (PAM)

JK Global IT Solutions offers PAM solutions to protect privileged accounts, enforce strong passwords, monitor sessions, employ just-in-time access controls, and implement least privilege principles.

Compliance & Regulatory Support

JK Global IT Solutions ensures clients meet cybersecurity regulations and standards, including GDPR, HIPAA, and PCI DSS. We provide tools for compliance audits and stay up-to-date on the latest regulations.
