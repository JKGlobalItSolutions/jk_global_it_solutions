Medical Coding

"JK Global IT Solutions provides expert medical coding services, accurately extracting and recording healthcare information from patient records, including procedures, diagnoses, equipment, and services. Partner with us to ensure precise and efficient medical coding for your healthcare needs."

Domestic

"JK Global IT Solutions offers Domestic BPO services, delivering tailored business process outsourcing solutions to enhance operational efficiency and drive growth within the local market. Our expertise ensures seamless support for your business needs, allowing you to focus on core activities while we handle the rest."

A domestic voice process is a type of BPO wherein a customer service agent deals with customers within the same country where the BPO is located.
